# Chapter 6 — Tools and Templates

This chapter documents the **20+ operational templates** covering the
full lifecycle of a digital-asset inheritance case — from client
acquisition through post-event audit. Templates are grouped into 10
functional categories aligned with the phases of the Sovereign Guard
Architecture.

All templates are **available in the companion
[Template Generator](../daio-unified/src/modules/template-generator)**
(free to use, multi-language DE/EN, export as Markdown/JSON).
White-label exports with custom branding require a commercial license.

## 6.1 Client Acquisition and Initial Consultation

The entry point. These templates structure first contact through
signed mandate.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Management Briefing Memo** | Internal alignment before client meeting |
| **Initial Consultation Questionnaire** | Discover client's digital-asset exposure |
| **Onboarding Checklist** | Standardized first-session structure |
| **Expectation Management Agreement** | Sets scope, timeline, deliverables |
| **Data Protection Consent Form** | GDPR-compliant processing authorization |

## 6.2 Asset Discovery and Documentation

Systematic identification and recording of all digital assets.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Digital Asset Inventory** | Master record of all identified assets |
| **Wallet Categorization Worksheet** | Self-custody vs. exchange vs. custodial classification |
| **Exchange Account Documentation** | Platform accounts, 2FA, recovery paths |
| **Asset Taxonomy Checklist** | Covers 8 categories — crypto, NFTs, gaming, creator economy, digital business, IP, cloud/identity, loyalty |
| **DeFi Position Mapping** | Smart contracts, LP positions, staking, yield farming |

## 6.3 Inheritance Container Creation

The core workflow — building the succession data structure.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Inheritance Container Structure** | Standard container schema with required fields |
| **Heir Profile Form** | Identity, relationship, entitlement share per heir |
| **Digital Will Addendum** | Integration into existing legal will |
| **Conditions Matrix** | Time limits, trigger conditions, stipulations |
| **Asset-to-Heir Mapping** | Allocation of specific assets to specific heirs |

## 6.4 Legacy Proof Protocol Documentation

Burden-of-proof reversal — validation during the owner's lifetime.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Proof Creation Checklist** | Systematic validation workflow |
| **Legacy Proof Certificate** | Cryptographically signed entitlement document |
| **Validation Update Log** | Regular updates to entitlement documentation |

## 6.5 Transfer Gate Processes

Multi-stage approval and authorization structures.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Transfer Gate Matrix** | Thresholds, approvers, escalation paths |
| **Notary Confirmation Template** | Formal notarial attestation |
| **Four-Eyes Approval Log** | Documented dual-authorization record |

## 6.6 Succession Sentinel Configuration

Automated monitoring and trigger configuration.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Sentinel Config** | Monitored data sources, escalation cascades |
| **Escalation Paths** | Primary → secondary → fallback notification chain |
| **Trigger Event Definition** | Configurable events beyond death (incapacity, age, life events) |

## 6.7 Estate Case Activation

Workflow when a trigger event occurs.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Activation Communication** | Standardized heir and stakeholder notification |
| **Case Status Dashboard** | Live tracking of active estate case |

## 6.8 Post-Event and Completion

Close-out procedures after successful transfer.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Completion Checklist** | Verification that all transfer steps are documented |
| **Satisfaction Survey** | Feedback from heirs and family |
| **Lessons-Learned Protocol** | Internal review for continuous improvement |

## 6.9 Compliance and Audit

Regulatory evidence and audit readiness.

| Template | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **MiCA Compliance Checklist** | EU 2023/1114 requirement mapping |
| **BGH Disclosure Obligation Log** | Evidence of III ZR 183/17 compliance |
| **BaFin Audit Trail Entry** | Formatted record per BaFin supervisory practice |
| **GDPR Data Subject Request Log** | Access, rectification, erasure tracking |
| **Retention Policy** | Document-class retention periods |

## 6.10 Master Template Overview

### Phase-to-Template Mapping

| Phase | Templates |
|---|---|
| **Phase 1 — Client Acquisition** | Management Briefing Memo, Initial Consultation, Onboarding, Expectation, GDPR Consent |
| **Phase 2 — Asset Discovery** | Digital Asset Inventory, Wallet Categorization, Exchange Documentation, Asset Taxonomy, DeFi Mapping |
| **Phase 3 — Container Creation** | Container Structure, Heir Profile, Digital Will Addendum, Conditions Matrix, Asset-Heir Mapping |
| **Phase 4 — Legacy Proof** | Proof Checklist, Proof Certificate, Validation Log |
| **Phase 5 — Transfer Gate** | Gate Matrix, Notary Confirmation, Four-Eyes Log |
| **Phase 6 — Sentinel Monitoring** | Sentinel Config, Escalation Paths, Trigger Definitions |
| **Phase 7 — Activation** | Activation Communication, Case Dashboard |
| **Phase 8 — Completion** | Completion Checklist, Satisfaction Survey, Lessons-Learned |
| **Phase 9 — Audit** | MiCA Checklist, BGH Log, BaFin Trail, GDPR Log, Retention Policy |

### Template Customization

All templates are designed for adaptation to the buyer's:

- **Firm branding** (logo, colors, typography) — white-label
- **Jurisdiction** (German, Austrian, Swiss, EU variants)
- **Client segment** (Family Office, private bank, notary, tax advisor)
- **Language** (DE/EN included by default; others extensible)
- **Internal governance** (approval thresholds, escalation paths,
  role definitions)
